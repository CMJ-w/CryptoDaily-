Candlestick Patterns

Candlestick patterns such as the Engulfing Bar, Doji, Morning Star, Hammer, and Shooting Star help traders predict market direction by understanding price psychology.

  • Engulfing Bar: A reversal signal showing shift in control between buyers and sellers.
  • Doji: Indicates indecision in the market.
  • Hammer: Bullish reversal after a downtrend.
  • Shooting Star: Bearish reversal after an uptrend.

Market Structure

Understanding market structure helps identify trends and entry points. The market can be trending, ranging, or choppy. Key tools include support/resistance and trendlines.

Trading Strategies

Use candlestick patterns along with market structure to execute trades. Major strategies include:

  • Pin Bar Strategy
  • Engulfing Bar Strategy
  • Inside Bar & False Breakout Strategy
  • Top-Down Analysis for Multi-Timeframe Confirmation

Money Management

Proper risk management protects capital and ensures long-term profitability. Never risk more than a small percentage per trade, and always use stop-loss and reward-to-risk planning.
